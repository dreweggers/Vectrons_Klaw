CCDC Team Takes 1st at State

February 23, 2017

This past Saturday, February 18th, SEMO’s CCDC Team took first place at the state level competition against Missouri S&T. The CCDC team will now be going to the Midwest Regional CCDC competition in Chicago, IL on March 17-18. Congratulations to all competitors and good luck at regionals!

If you’re interested in joining the CCDC team next year, contact Lucas Kossack at [email protected] or Jonathan Fulling at [email protected]

New meeting location

August 23, 2016

With Cybersecurity moving under the Computer Science department, all the CY classes have been moved to the new Cybersecurity classroom, Dempster 128.  We’ll also be holding our meetings there on Wednesdays at noon, as usual, starting August 31st.

Cyber Defense Club Meetings

August 23, 2015

Attention all students who are interested in cybersecurity! We will have weekly club meetings on Wednesdays at noon in the Polytech building rm #105.

In the club we discuss a variety of topics related to cybersecurity. The club allows students to learn more than what is taught in the classroom. It is also an excellent way to meet other students who have the same interests as you! Come and check it out.

Second Year: Second Place

March 31, 2014

A team of 9 students (8 competitors and 1 alternate) competed in the Midwest Regional Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition this weekend for the 2nd time in 2 years of competing.

It was a hard fought competition but we came out in 2nd place.  This is a tremendous accomplishment for our team.

Congratulations everyone.  Great Job!
